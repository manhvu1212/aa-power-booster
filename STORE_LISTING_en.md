## Full description — max 4000 characters
```
AA Power Booster lets you easily control your car's throttle-response controller (electronic throttle sensor) over Bluetooth — right on your phone or on your car's Android Auto screen.

★ 5 DRIVING MODES
• Race – instant throttle response, the most aggressive
• Sport – sporty, quick acceleration
• Normal – the car's original (stock) response
• City – smooth and comfortable for city driving
• Eco – fuel saving

★ KEY FEATURES
• Adjust throttle sensitivity across 9 levels for each mode
• Each mode remembers its own level – switching modes restores the level you set
• Android Auto support: pick a mode and adjust the level right on the car screen
• Direct control on your phone with a clean, modern interface
• Automatically reconnects to the saved device when you open the app
• Real-time status sync with the device

★ PRIVATE & LIGHTWEIGHT
• Does not collect personal data
• No Internet permission, no ads, no tracking
• Small size, runs smoothly

★ REQUIREMENTS
The app needs to connect to a compatible throttle-response controller (a "JDY"-type Bluetooth module) already installed in your car. This is a controller app and does not include the hardware device.

★ ABOUT PERMISSIONS
• Bluetooth: to scan for and connect to the throttle controller.
• Location: Android requires this permission to allow Bluetooth scanning on some versions. The app does NOT collect or use your location.

⚠ Safety: Please make adjustments while the car is stopped, or let a passenger operate it. Always drive safely.
```
